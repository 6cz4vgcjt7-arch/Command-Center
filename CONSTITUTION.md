# Seasons Constitution

## 1. Weekly Review is the heart of Seasons.
If a feature does not strengthen the Weekly Review, it should be challenged.

## 2. Seasons is a weekly financial habit app.
The product exists to help people build lasting financial achievements through one consistent weekly practice.

## 3. Seasons never judges. Seasons observes.
The app should state facts clearly and calmly. It should never shame, grade, or scold.

## 4. Manual reflection is a feature.
Entering balances is not a limitation. It is the act that creates awareness.

## 5. Reflection comes before planning.
Record first. Notice what changed. Then decide what deserves focus.

## 6. Achievements are earned in life, not awarded by the app.
Seasons recognizes meaningful progress without turning personal finance into a game.

## 7. Calm is a feature.
No ads. No noisy notifications. No engagement tricks. No unnecessary urgency.

## 8. Data belongs to the user.
Version 1 should remain local-first and privacy-focused.

## 9. Remove complexity before adding features.
If removing something does not make the experience worse, it should not be there.

## 10. Seasons should feel like opening a beautifully crafted notebook.
The app should feel calm, deliberate, and trustworthy — not like operating financial software.
