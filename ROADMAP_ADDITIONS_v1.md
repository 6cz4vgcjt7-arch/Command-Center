# Seasons v1.0 Foundation Additions

## Locked Concepts
- Commitments live inside the Weekly Review.
- Commitments persist until completed.
- Tap a Commitment to view its Projection.
- Projection shows payoff acceleration, interest savings, and timeline impact.
- Guided Reflection is event-driven, not constant.
- Discover Your Current Season replaces a simple season picker.
- Preserve is part of a cycle, not an ending.
- Journal-first design.
- Bookmark ribbon for Commitments.
- Open-arc leaf logo.
- Thin elegant progress rings that gain visual weight as progress increases.
