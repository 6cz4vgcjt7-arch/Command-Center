# Commitments (Locked)

Purpose:
Remember intentional decisions made to your future self.

Lifecycle
1. Create
2. Remember (shown each Weekly Review)
3. View Projection
4. Commitment Check-In
5. Resolve

Resolution States
- Completed
- Revised
- Archived
- Explained

Commitment Check-In

If a trigger has occurred but the commitment has not been fulfilled:

Three weeks ago you planned to redirect $500/month to Chase.

What would you like to do?

- I've completed it
- Update my plan
- Tell Future Me why it changed

Principles
- Seasons remembers.
- Seasons asks.
- Seasons reflects.
- Seasons never shames.
- Every commitment deserves an ending.
